Conversion Formulas

Length

  • 1 kilometer (km) = 1000 meters (m)
  • 1 meter (m) = 100 centimeters (cm)
  • 1 meter (m) = 1000 millimeters (mm)
  • 1 inch (in) = 2.54 centimeters (cm)
  • 1 mile (mi) = 1.60934 kilometers (km)
  • 1 yard (yd) = 0.9144 meters (m)
  • 1 foot (ft) = 0.3048 meters (m)

Temperature

  • Celsius to Fahrenheit: (°C × 9/5) + 32 = °F
  • Fahrenheit to Celsius: (°F - 32) × 5/9 = °C
  • Celsius to Kelvin: °C + 273.15 = K
  • Kelvin to Celsius: K - 273.15 = °C
  • Fahrenheit to Kelvin: ((°F - 32) × 5/9) + 273.15 = K
  • Kelvin to Fahrenheit: ((K - 273.15) × 9/5) + 32 = °F

Area

  • 1 square meter (m²) = 10.7639 square feet (ft²)
  • 1 square foot (ft²) = 0.092903 square meters (m²)
  • 1 acre = 4046.86 square meters (m²)
  • 1 hectare = 10,000 square meters (m²)
  • 1 square kilometer (km²) = 1,000,000 square meters (m²)
  • 1 square mile (mi²) = 2.58999 square kilometers (km²)

Volume

  • 1 liter (L) = 1000 milliliters (mL)
  • 1 milliliter (mL) = 0.001 liters (L)
  • 1 gallon (gal) = 3.78541 liters (L)
  • 1 liter (L) = 0.264172 gallons (gal)
  • 1 cubic meter (m³) = 1000 liters (L)
  • 1 cubic inch (in³) = 16.387 cubic centimeters (cm³)
  • 1 cubic foot (ft³) = 0.0283168 cubic meters (m³)

Weight

  • 1 kilogram (kg) = 1000 grams (g)
  • 1 gram (g) = 0.001 kilograms (kg)
  • 1 pound (lb) = 0.453592 kilograms (kg)
  • 1 kilogram (kg) = 2.20462 pounds (lb)
  • 1 ounce (oz) = 28.3495 grams (g)
  • 1 gram (g) = 0.035274 ounces (oz)
  • 1 stone (st) = 6.35029 kilograms (kg)
